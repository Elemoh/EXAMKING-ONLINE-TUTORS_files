Your Right to Be Forgotten

1. Requesting Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting our support team.

2. Verification Process

We will verify your identity before processing deletion requests for security reasons.

3. Timeframe

Deletion requests are processed within 14 business days.

4. Exceptions

We may retain data where legally required (e.g., financial or security records).

5. Confirmation

After deletion, you will receive a confirmation email. This action is permanent and cannot be reversed.

6. Contact

To request deletion, email us at: support@mrsurest.com
